Our history

Welcome to the inspiring story of Rainydays, a company dedicated to revolutionizing rainwear for individuals aged 30 to 50. Founded with a vision and passion for redefining how people experience rainy days, Rainydays has become a beacon of style, comfort, and innovation in the world of raincoats.

Inception and Ambition

Rainydays was born in the vibrant city of Rainford, where rain was as common as sunshine in other places. The founder, Amanda Bennett, had a dream to provide rainwear that transcended utility, catering to the tastes and lifestyles of those in the prime of their lives. Armed with this vision, she took the plunge, and Rainydays was born.

Customer-Centric Approach

Understanding the unique preferences of individuals aged 30 to 50 became Rainydays' mantra. The company embarked on a journey of constant engagement with its customers, gathering invaluable feedback to refine its designs. This dedication to customer satisfaction became a cornerstone of Rainydays' success.

Sustainable Commitment

In an era of growing environmental consciousness, Rainydays embraced sustainability. They introduced eco-friendly materials and manufacturing practices, aligning with the values of their customers. Rainydays' commitment to sustainability not only benefited the planet but also resonated with their loyal clientele.

Expanding Horizons

With a growing reputation for style and quality, Rainydays expanded its product range to include rain boots, umbrellas, and waterproof accessories that perfectly complemented their iconic raincoats. The brand became synonymous with comprehensive rain solutions for the modern, discerning customer.

Today and Beyond

Rainydays is no longer just a raincoat company; it's a lifestyle brand.

With a robust online presence and brick-and-mortar stores in major cities, Rainydays continues to inspire and protect. Its products have graced the shoulders of countless individuals, turning rainy days into opportunities for style expression.

As Rainydays looks to the future, its commitment to age-defying style and uncompromising quality remains unwavering. The brand's journey, rooted in the vision of Amanda Bennett, has reshaped the way people think about rainwear, proving that age 30 to 50 is a period of life that deserves to be celebrated - even on the rainiest of days.

Experience the Rainydays difference today, where rainwear is more than just protection; it's a fashion statement, a lifestyle choice, and a testament to the enduring spirit of those who refuse to let the weather dictate their style.
